NewJeans Entire Members

NewJeans (뉴진스) is a 5-member girl group under ADOR and HYBE Labels. The members consist of Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. They released their debut single “Attention” on July 22, 2022, followed by their debut extended play, New Jeans, which was released on August 1, 2022.

HANNI PHAM

Hanni (하니) is a Vietnamese-Australian singer and member of NewJeans.

Hanni was born on October 6, 2004, in Melbourne, Australia. Her father is from Hanoi, Vietnam, and her mother is from Ho Chi Minh City (also known as Saigon).

How did NewJeans get its start?

Despite their fame and luxury brand partnerships, much of NewJeans’ popularity seems to stem from their girl-next-door relatability. For instance, many of their early TikToks appear as though they were filmed by an amateur, with fuzzy video quality and unpolished transitions.

While NewJeans is popular as a group, the girls are also rising stars in their own right. For instance, CNN reports that Louis Vuitton recently signed Hyein, Danielle joined Burberry as a model, Hanni is a model for Gucci and Armani Beauty and Minji is with Chanel.
